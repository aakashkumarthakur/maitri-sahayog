Providing Clean Drinking Water

Installation of 4 Bore-Well Water Pumps in Malangwa Municipality

Support This Project

About Maitri Sahayog Sanstha

Non-profit organization dedicated to community welfare. Our focus areas include health, clean water access, and social development in underserved regions of Nepal.

Project Overview

Location
Malangwa Municipality, Sarlahi District, Nepal
Key Highlights
4 Bore-Well Pumps
800 Direct Beneficiaries
Focus Wards
Wards 3, 7, 9, 11

The Problem

  • Waterborne diseases (Diarrhea, Cholera, Typhoid)
  • Lack of safe drinking water
  • Women & children walking long distances for water

Project Objectives

  • Provide clean drinking water access
  • Reduce waterborne diseases
  • Save time for women & children
  • Promote community participation

Project Locations

  • Ward 11 – Palsi
  • Ward 7 – Musaili
  • Ward 3 – Malangwa
  • Ward 9 – Malangwa
